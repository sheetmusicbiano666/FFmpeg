Convert Video to Photo/Image
1 Step: Enter "Win+R" and enter "cmd"
2 Step: Enter "ffmpeg"
3 Step: Enter "ffmpeg -i Nerwitch66.mp4 image%05d.png"
End

Convert Photo/Image to Video
1 Step: Enter "Win+R" and enter "cmd"
2 Step: Enter "ffmpeg"
3 Step: Enter "ffmpeg -r 60 -i image%05d.png -pix_fmt yuv420p Zenithwirch666.mp4"
End

Add Audio in Video
1 Step: Enter "Win+R" and enter "cmd"
2 Step: Enter "ffmpeg"
3 Step: Enter "ffmpeg -i Vindel66.mp3 -i Zenithwirch666.mp4 Convertttt66.mp4"
End
